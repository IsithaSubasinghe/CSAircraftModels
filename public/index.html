Welcome to CS Aircraft Models, where every aircraft is brought to life through the art of true craftsmanship. Each piece is fully handmade, hand-carved, and hand-painted by a single master craftsman, ensuring unmatched attention to detail and character. Proudly made in Sri Lanka, our pieces are designed for collectors, aviation lovers, and anyone who appreciates excellent craftsmanship.

THE PLANE MAN

Meet the Artist Behind Every Aircraft

Shayanaka Liyanage

Recognized by national media as ‘The Plane Man’

For over a decade, handcrafted aircraft have emerged from a single, meticulous bench — each a study in nuance and patience. This is the work of a one-man workshop where every surface is considered and every detail matters.

The process is driven by precision and an insistence on refinement. From the curvature of a fuselage to the finish of a miniature cockpit, each model is a quiet testament to craftsmanship and care.

What you see is the result of passion translated into objects that look as though they could have flown. Presented here, the models become artifacts — part museum, part personal archive, fully human.
